Imagine Dragons

Pop Rock, Alternative Rock, Alternative Pop, Electronic Rock

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band formed in Las Vegas, Nevada, in 2008. The original lineup consisted of lead vocalist Dan Reynolds, guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman. Reynolds, born in Las Vegas, had a deep passion for music from a young age. He met Sermon while studying at Brigham Young University, where they started collaborating on music.
